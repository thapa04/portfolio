Photobooth

Me and two other group members, Anirudh and Brian worked together in Mr.Browns CSP class to engineer an app. With the assistance of MIT inventor we created a photobooth app that is downloadable for android. Our app allowed the user take up to four images, color the images and share it on email. We made lots of modifications to the original adding different color options and customizations. As a group we faced many challenges. We encountered problems with the image facing the correct direction and resetting the canvas. However we collaborated well and were able to over come these challenges. We all had responsibilities on this project, mine was to work on the projects code and collaborate with Anirudh on ideas for it. I took a lot away from working on this, It taught me how to overcome obstacles, stay persistent, and teamwork management.
