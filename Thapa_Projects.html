Hydrogen Solar Cell Car

November 2019 - Principles of Engineering
Click here to view our full documentation

Me and my team of 4 consisting of Anish Kolan, Shrijith Vakula, and Andrew Jayasinge worked to create a car powered only from hydrogen or solar cells. We applied our skills of simple machines, and energy to create an efficient car through vex parts as well as a vex motor. The 3 wheeled car car has a big to small gear ratio to maximize speed. The largest front wheel is spun though our gears and motor, dragging the back two wheels. For our car, we used only solar energy as we found hydrogen cells to add too much weight. Overall our speed was 4.35 seconds in one meter. While designing and testing our car, we encountered many challenges. One of them was finding away to manage our limited space that was given to us. We found it difficult to fit in two solar panels while making a longer and less wide car. Overall, I have learned a lot from this project, most importantly, the process of trial and error and the perseverance involved when designing something.
