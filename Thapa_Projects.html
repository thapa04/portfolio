MIT App Inventor Photobooth

September 2018-Computer Science Principals
Click here to view our full documentation

Me and two other group members, Anirudh and Brian worked together in Mr.Browns CSP class to engineer an app. With the assistance of MIT inventor we created a photobooth app that is downloadable for android. Our app allowed the user take up to four images, color the images and share it on email. We made lots of modifications to the original adding different color options and customizations. As a group we faced many challenges. We encountered problems with the image facing the incorrect direction and resetting the canvas. However we collaborated well and were able to over come these challenges. We all had responsibilities on this project, mine was to work on the projects code and collaborate with Anirudh on ideas for it. I took a lot away from working on this, It taught me how to overcome obstacles, stay persistent, and team management.

Scratch Tera Platform Game

September 2018-Computer Science Principals
Click here to view our full documentation

Me and my partner Advaith worked together in Mr Browns CSP class to program a platforming game using scratch. Our app uses a ghost sprite that floats around levels.The player must avoid the lava and hit the yellow beam. Unfortunately we did not have time to perfect it as much as we wanted to and we found the jump mechanics to be a bit buggy. We intended it to be a multiplayer game but never got to that stage in the game. My role was programming the scratch and creating the levels. I helped a little with writing as well. Doing this taught me a lot about the fundamentals of coding and what is required in them. I also learned how to stay patient when coding During this project me and my partner had to collaborate a lot to reach our goals we set.
